Bianca Mounce

Friday, April 4, 2015

<p>Using Data in the Non-Profit World: Bianca Mounce has worked in the non-profit advocacy space, leveraging data analysis for strategic decision-making. Most recently, she served as National Data Manager for Organizing for Action -- a community organizing non-profit that advocates for President Obama's agenda. Bianca will talk about how OFA utilized data in order to be smarter organizers and the role that data can play in advocacy.</p>
