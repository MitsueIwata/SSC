About

South Side Civic is a bi-weekly hack night for policy wonks, coders, developers, urbanists, ruralists, activists, analysts, scientists, sociologists, civic/social-minded entrepreneurs, organizers, mathematicians, statisticians, teachers, students, and, generally, people who want to work with the community to learn and do some civic hacking.


We are a CAPP student-led organization interested in policy, civic leadership, and tech innovation.  Contact us if you have any questions or comments you'd like to share!
