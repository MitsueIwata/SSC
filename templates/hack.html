About the event

South Side Civic is partnering with UChicago’s Institute of Politics (IOP), Department of Computer Science, and the Harris School for the first time to host a two-day Civic Hackathon!

Our civic hackathon may more appropriately be called a scope-athon. The main objective is to take a civic-minded or community-based problem and to form committed groups to scope out the problem, breaking it down into its parts and developing a plan to address the problem. The solutions may or may not be entirely tech-oriented, or only parts of the solution may have a tech component.

Our civic hackathon provides the opportunity to gain problem-scoping skills, learn about other civic-tech organizations, network with community leaders, and to join a team and enjoy the thrill and excitement of a 2-day hackathon.

What is Our Goal?

The event will bring together leaders of civic organizations, students, and community members to learn about civic hacking, civic problems, and how to work in teams to form a solution. Participants will come from various backgrounds, including, but not limited to, computer science, political science and public policy, economics, geography, visual art, civic innovation and leadership, etc.

  • Learn and exercise problem-scoping skills
  • Meet civic leaders in active organizations
  • Work on real problems
  • Become a part of a civic-minded tech community

Keynote Speaker - Aneesh Chopra


CTRL+ALT+DLT: How Civic Hackers are Changing the World

Aneesh Chopra, the nation’s first Chief Technology Officer, will share his insights on the power of tech to connect, engage and inspire civic participation. Mr. Chopra will draw on his experiences as America’s CTO and will discuss the power of technology and human innovation to solve some of our most vexing civic problems in this keynote address, which kicks off the Civic Hackathon. Mr. Chopra will be joined by City of Chicago’s Chief Data Officer, Tom Schenk, to continue the discussion. This address is made possible with the generous support of JP Morgan Chase. RSVP here
