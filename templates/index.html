Welcome

At South Side Civic we hope to build a community of south siders interested in open data and its civic applications. We bring together students, community members, and leaders of civic organizations to learn about civic problems and how to work together to solve them. Our hack nights are held concurrently with the UChicago's hack night at the Chicago Innovation Exchange on 53rd street from 5-8pm.
