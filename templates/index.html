Welcome!

At South Side Civic we hope to build a community of south siders interested in open data and its civic applications. We bring together students, community members, and leaders of civic organizations to learn about civic problems and how to work together to solve them.

Participants come from various backgrounds, including, but not limited to, computer science, political science and public policy, economics, geography, visual art, civic innovation and leadership, etc.
